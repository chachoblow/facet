# List markers

Bulleted with hyphens:

- alpha
- beta
- gamma

Bulleted with asterisks:

* alpha
* beta
* gamma

Bulleted with plus:

+ alpha
+ beta
+ gamma

Ordered with periods:

1. one
2. two
3. three

Ordered with parens:

1) one
2) two
3) three

Nested mixed:

- outer hyphen
  * inner asterisk
    + deepest plus
      1. ordered period
      2) ordered paren
